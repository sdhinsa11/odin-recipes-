Pizza

This is a classic dish that everyone loves. With just some dough, tomato sauce and any meat, veggies and cheese. Everyone will come together too enjoy this meal!

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Pizza dough or flatbread
  • shredded cheese
  • onions, jalapenos, green peppers, mushrooms, any veggies
  • pepperoni or ham or no meat

Steps:

  1. roll out the dough (skip this step if you have flatbread)
  2. spread your pizza sauce all around the dough with a spoon, but leaving the edges without any sauce
  3. sprinkle as much cheese all over the dough
  4. add the toppings of your choosing
  5. put your pizza in the oven and bake
  6. enjoy!
