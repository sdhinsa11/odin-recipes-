Smoothie

Whether you just finished a workout, or it is hot outside or you want too drink something refreshing then a Smoothie is just for you! With a blend of fruit, milk or juice and ice you will have the perfect afternoon or morning cool down!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk or juice
  • Any fruit you want (I like using frozen fruit)
  • Lots of ice (optional)

Steps:

  1. Start by putting your liquid in first into a blender
  2. Add your fruit
  3. if you like it cold then add in ice ()
  4. Blend it all up and enjoy!
